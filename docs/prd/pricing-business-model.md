**ROI Calculation:**

## Pricing Psychology

### Why $29/Month Works

**Anchoring:**
- Competitors: Otter ($17), Folk CRM ($20), Fireflies ($10)
- MeetSolis at $29 = premium positioning
- But still <$50 threshold (impulse buy for B2B)

**Value Perception:**
- Free tier establishes trust (no credit card required)
- $29 feels like "professional tool", not "cheap app"
- Annual discount (save $99) = tangible savings

**Conversion Triggers:**
- Free limit (3 clients, 3 meetings) = low enough to hit quickly
- But high enough to prove value first
- Upgrade prompt: "You've used 3/3 clients. Upgrade for 50 clients."

### Retention Strategies

**Onboarding (First 7 Days):**
- Email sequence: Day 1 (welcome), Day 3 (first client guide), Day 7 (upgrade benefits)
- In-app nudges: "Add your first client in 30 seconds"
- Success milestone: Users who add 2+ clients have 80% retention

**Engagement (Ongoing):**
- Weekly summary email: "You had 3 meetings this week, 5 action items completed"
- Prepare reminders: "You have a meeting with Client X tomorrow. Prepare now?"

## Pricing FAQ

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?**
A: Yes! Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your billing period.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my limits?**
- Free plan: You can still upload meetings manually (unlimited). Or upgrade to Pro.
- Pro plan: We'll notify you and offer add-ons (e.g., $1 per extra AI meeting).

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. Bank-level encryption, we never train AI on your data, you can delete everything anytime.

**Q: Can I try Pro before paying?**
A: Yes! 14-day free trial, no credit card required.

**Q: What if I need more than 20 meetings/month?**
A: Manual uploads are unlimited (you transcribe). For AI transcription, we offer add-ons ($1/meeting) or contact us for custom plans.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes, 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.
