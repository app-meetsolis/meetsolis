# Non-Goals

**Version:** 3.0
**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

This document defines what MeetSolis will NOT build for the MVP (March 2026) or short-term future. These are deliberate scope boundaries, not oversights.

---

## Product Non-Goals

### Not a Video Platform
MeetSolis does not build, host, or replace video conferencing infrastructure. Coaches use Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, or any tool they prefer. MeetSolis captures intelligence *after* meetings, not during them.

### Not a Real-Time Meeting Bot
No in-meeting bots, no live transcription, no screen/audio capture during meetings. MeetSolis is a post-meeting intelligence tool. Coaches upload transcripts manually or via file after sessions.

*(Note: Async auto-transcription of uploaded audio/video files IS in scope — this is different from real-time capture.)*

### Not a CRM
MeetSolis is not a sales CRM, pipeline manager, or deal tracker. Client cards are coaching-specific intelligence containers, not lead/opportunity records.

### Not a Practice Management Platform
Scheduling, invoicing, client portals, progress tracking dashboards, habit trackers — not in scope. Tools like Practice Better and Simply.Coach cover this space. MeetSolis complements them with AI-powered session memory.

### Not a Collaboration Tool
Teams, shared workspaces, multi-seat accounts, permission systems — all post-MVP. MVP serves solo executive coaches only.

### Not Therapist/HIPAA-Compliant (Explicitly Out)
MeetSolis does not support therapists, psychologists, or any regulated mental health practice requiring HIPAA compliance. This is a deliberate product decision. If HIPAA is required, MeetSolis is not the right tool.

### Not an Automation Platform
Calendar sync, auto-import from Zoom/Google Meet, automated reminders, webhook integrations, Zapier — post-MVP or never.

## Why Narrow Scope Matters

The executive coaching market is narrow (30,000 coaches in English-speaking markets) but high-value ($99/month feels cheap against $200–500/hr billing). Winning this niche deeply is more valuable than broad, shallow coverage. Every feature not built is focus preserved for the core loop: **upload session → get AI summary → query history → stay relevant to your clients.**
