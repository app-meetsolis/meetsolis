# Story 5.6: Landing Page (Executive Coach Positioning)

## Status
Not Started

## Story

**As a** potential customer (independent executive coach),
**I want** a landing page that immediately speaks to my specific situation,
**so that** I know within 10 seconds that this tool was built for me.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Landing page at `/` clearly targets executive coaches in hero copy
2. H1: "Never forget a client's breakthrough moment again."
3. Subheadline explains value: AI memory for coaching sessions, instant recall across all clients
4. Primary CTA: "Start Free — No Credit Card Required" → `/sign-up`
5. "How It Works" section: 3 clear steps (Upload → Summary → Ask Solis)
6. Features section: 4 key features (Session Memory, Solis Intelligence, Action Items, Auto-Transcription)
7. Pricing section: Free (3 clients, 5 sessions, 75 queries) vs Pro ($99/month, unlimited clients, 25 sessions, 2,000 queries)
8. Trust signals section: ICF-aligned, data privacy, built exclusively for executive coaches
9. Footer: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Contact links
10. Page loads in <2 seconds
11. Mobile responsive — hero, features, and pricing readable on mobile
12. This replaces or significantly updates the v1 landing page from Story 1.7

## Page Structure

### Hero

```
Subheadline: "MeetSolis gives executive coaches perfect recall across every
              client's coaching journey — so you walk into every session
              fully prepared, every time."
CTA:         [Start Free — No Credit Card Required] [See How It Works ↓]
```

### How It Works (3 Steps)
1. **Upload your session transcript** — Text file, Word doc, paste, or audio recording
2. **Get an AI summary in seconds** — Key insights, topics, and action items extracted automatically
3. **Ask Solis anything** — "What is Sarah's biggest challenge right now?" — answered in <5 seconds with citations

### Features (4 Cards)
- **Session Memory** — Every coaching session, searchable forever. Never lose context between sessions.
- **Solis Intelligence** — Ask questions about any client's history. Get cited answers from real session data.
- **Action Item Tracking** — Auto-extract and track commitments from coach and client. Mark complete with one click.
- **Auto-Transcription** — Upload audio or video recordings. Deepgram's speaker diarization identifies coach vs. client automatically.

### Pricing
```
FREE                    PRO — $99/month or $948/year
3 active clients        Unlimited clients
5 lifetime AI sessions  25 AI sessions/month
75 lifetime queries     2,000 Solis queries/month
Unlimited manual upload ICF-aligned documentation
                        Priority support (24hr response)

[Start Free]            [Upgrade to Pro]
```

### Trust Signals
- "ICF-aligned session documentation standards"
- "Your data is never used to train AI models"
- "Built exclusively for executive coaches — not a generic transcription tool"
